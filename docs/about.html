About Sister Blooms

Sister Blooms creates bright, modern bouquets for birthdays, anniversaries, celebrations, and everyday surprises. We keep ordering simple: choose a bouquet, customise your options, and confirm your order on WhatsApp.

What we offer

  • Seasonal bouquets with clean wrapping
  • Custom colours and sizes (depending on stock)
  • Add-ons: chocolates, card note, vase (where available)
  • Local delivery in selected Cape Town areas

How ordering works

  1. Browse bouquets and choose your favourite
  2. Customise size, colour, and add-ons
  3. Add to cart and review your order
  4. Send your order on WhatsApp from the cart
