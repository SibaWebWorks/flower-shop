About Sister Blooms

Sister Blooms creates bright, modern bouquets for birthdays, anniversaries, celebrations, and everyday surprises. We keep ordering simple: choose a bouquet, customise your options, and confirm your order on WhatsApp.

What we offer

  • Seasonal bouquets with clean wrapping
  • Custom colours and sizes (depending on stock)
  • Add-ons: chocolates, card note, vase (where available)
  • Local delivery in selected Cape Town areas

How ordering works

  1. Browse bouquets and choose your favourite
  2. Customise size, colour, and add-ons
  3. Add to cart and review your order
  4. Send your order on WhatsApp from the cart

Delivery & Ordering Policy

Back to top

Delivery areas

We deliver to selected areas within Cape Town. Delivery availability and fees depend on distance and are confirmed on WhatsApp.

Delivery times

Operating hours: Mon–Sat, 08:00–18:00. Same-day slots may be available depending on stock and your area.

Ordering & payment

Orders are placed via WhatsApp (from your cart). We confirm bouquet availability, delivery options, delivery fee, and the final total before payment. Prices may vary slightly based on seasonal flowers and availability.

Returns & substitutions

Because flowers are perishable, returns are not accepted. If a specific flower or colour is unavailable, we may suggest a suitable substitution of equal or greater value.
