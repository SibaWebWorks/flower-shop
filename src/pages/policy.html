Policy

Clear, simple terms so you know what to expect. We confirm availability and final totals on WhatsApp.

Ordering

  • Add your bouquets to cart and send one order on WhatsApp.
  • We confirm availability, substitutions (if needed), and your final total before delivery.
  • Same-day slots depend on area + time of order.

Delivery

  • Delivery fees depend on distance and are confirmed on WhatsApp.
  • We’ll confirm available delivery windows before you pay.
  • For areas outside our usual zones, we’ll advise lead time or alternatives.

Substitutions

  • Flowers are seasonal. If a stem is unavailable, we may suggest a similar alternative.
  • We’ll always confirm substitutions on WhatsApp before finalising.

Payments

  • Payment details are shared on WhatsApp after we confirm your order.
  • Your order is only scheduled once payment is confirmed (unless agreed otherwise).

Refunds & cancellations

  • If you need to cancel, message us as soon as possible on WhatsApp.
  • Because flowers are perishable, refunds depend on preparation and delivery stage.
  • We’ll handle issues fairly—share a photo within a reasonable time after delivery.
